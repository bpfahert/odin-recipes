Lasagna!!!!!

Make the tasty lasagna for your cats! Cats love lasagna!

Use one cheese and two beefs to make the lasagna. Feed to cat.

Ingredients!

  • Pasta
  • Beef
  • Cheese!

Steps!

  1. Add cheese, pasta, and beef to baking pan.
  2. Put in oven for some time.
  3. Let cool and feed to cat!aaaaa
